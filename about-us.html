About Us

Who We Are

Opangi is a company dedicated to providing engaging online games for memory training and brain development. Our goal is to create fun and effective games that help users improve their cognitive abilities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help individuals enhance their memory, focus, and mental agility through interactive gaming experiences. We believe that learning and improving should be enjoyable!

Our Team

Our team consists of game developers, designers, and researchers passionate about mental wellness and cognitive training.
