How to Play Wii to Lose Weight

Step 1: Become a Mii
Get Wii Fit and follow the instructions to set up a Mii (a virtual alter ego) that will track your fitness stats and record your progress.

Step 2: Do Aerobic Exercise
Focus on aerobic exercise. Yoga and balance games can help you tone up, and strength training can build muscle so you burn calories more efficiently, but aerobic exercise is the most direct way to lose weight.

Step 3: Start Dancing
Start boogying along to "Dance Dance Revolution": Studies show that people can burn about 172 calories per half hour in light mode, 201 calories in standard mode, and 243 calories in hard mode.
Tip: Find a dance partner and challenge yourself to a virtual dance contest every day.

Step 4: Exercise
If dancing isn't your thing, try Wii Sports. One study found that Wii boxing burned 216 calories every 30 minutes. Tennis was next at 159 calories per half hour, and then baseball at 135 calories per 30 minutes.
Tip: Golf burned the least calories, at 93 calories per half hour, followed by bowling at 117 calories per 30 minutes.

Step 5: Follow the Program
Type "Wii weight loss plan" into a search engine and you'll find weight loss plans from people who have successfully used the Wii to lose weight.

Step 6: Play Regularly
Play every day if you can. Because Wii games burn fewer calories than real-life activities, you need to play regularly to see results.
Fact: A couple lost a combined 117 pounds playing Dance Dance Revolution.
